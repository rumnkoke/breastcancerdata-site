Why we're building this

When you're diagnosed with breast cancer, you need information immediately. But what's available is fragmented, clinical, or designed for researchers — not for patients making the biggest decisions of their lives.

Breast Cancer Data exists to change that. We're creating a place where the patient voice is centered, where complexity becomes clarity, and where a frightening diagnosis becomes something you can understand, discuss with your care team, and make informed decisions around.

This started with one family's experience. It grows with yours.
